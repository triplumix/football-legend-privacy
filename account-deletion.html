Account Deletion

How to delete your Football Legend account and data

Delete from Inside the App

1. Open Football Legend on your device.

2. Tap the ⚙️ Settings icon (gear) on the Home screen.

3. Scroll down to the Danger Zone section.

4. Tap "Delete Account".

5. Confirm the deletion when prompted.

What Gets Deleted

  • Your Firebase authentication account (email/password credentials)
  • All cloud save data stored in Firebase Firestore
  • Your user profile information
  • All associated data on our servers

Note: Local game data stored on your device is not automatically removed. To clear local data, uninstall the App or clear its storage via your device settings.

Request Deletion via Email

If you cannot access the App, you may request account deletion by emailing us at stefan.stefanica@ymail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request". Include the email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 7 business days.

Data Retained After Deletion

  • Aggregated analytics: Anonymous, aggregated analytics data that cannot identify you may be retained.
  • Crash reports: Historical crash reports in Sentry are retained for up to 90 days and cannot be individually deleted, but they contain only technical data (stack traces, device info).
  • Purchase records: Transaction records held by Apple or Google are governed by their respective policies.
