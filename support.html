Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a new game?

Tap "New Game" on the main menu, choose your country and club, then tap "Start". You'll begin as the club chairman and manage everything from transfers to finances.

Do I need an internet connection to play?

No. Football Legend works fully offline. An internet connection is only needed for cloud saves, leaderboards, and the in-app shop.

How do I restore my purchases?

Go to the Shop screen and tap "Restore Purchases" at the bottom. This will restore any previously purchased non-consumable items (like Legendary Powers).

How do I back up my save data?

Go to Settings and sign in with your account. Your progress will be automatically synced to the cloud. You can restore it on any device by signing in with the same account.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings → scroll down → tap "Delete Account". This will permanently remove your account and all associated cloud data. Learn more.

I purchased something but didn't receive it. What do I do?

First, try restarting the app and checking your internet connection. If the issue persists, try "Restore Purchases" in the Shop. If it still doesn't work, email us at support@triplumix.com with your Apple receipt or transaction ID.
