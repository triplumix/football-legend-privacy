Football Legend — Mobile Game Application

Effective Date: March 16, 2026  |  Last Updated: March 16, 2026

Summary: Football Legend is a football management simulation game. We respect your privacy. The App collects minimal data necessary for functionality, advertising, and crash reporting. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

1. Introduction

Football Legend ("the App", "our App") is a mobile football management simulation game available on the Google Play Store. The App is developed and operated by Stefan Stefanica ("we", "us", "our", "the Developer").

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, share, and protect information when you download, install, and use Football Legend. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the App.

This Privacy Policy applies to all users of Football Legend, regardless of location or device.

2. Information We Collect

We are committed to collecting only the minimum amount of data necessary to provide and improve the App. Below is a detailed breakdown of the types of information that may be collected:

2.1 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device model, manufacturer, operating system version, screen resolution, language settings, and unique device identifiers (such as Android Advertising ID).
  • App Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the App, including session duration, screens visited, features used, game actions performed, and in-game events.
  • Crash and Performance Data: When the App encounters an error, we collect crash logs, stack traces, device state at the time of the crash, and performance metrics to help us identify and fix bugs.
  • Network Information: IP address (which may be used to approximate general location at the country or city level), connection type (Wi-Fi or mobile data), and carrier information.
  • Advertising Data: Ad interaction data such as ads viewed, ads clicked, and advertising identifiers used to serve and measure the effectiveness of advertisements.

2.2 Information Stored Locally

  • Game Save Data: All game progress, including your team roster, match history, league standings, financial records, and user preferences are stored locally on your device.
  • Settings and Preferences: Your app configuration choices, such as notification preferences, sound settings, and display options.

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any personally identifiable information directly.
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, camera, microphone, or files.
  • We do not require you to create an account or log in to use the App.
  • We do not collect location data (GPS) from your device.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used for the following purposes:

  • App Functionality: To provide, operate, and maintain the core features of Football Legend, including game mechanics, save/load functionality, and user interface.
  • Performance Improvement: To analyze app performance, identify bottlenecks, optimize loading times, and improve the overall user experience.
  • Bug Fixing: To detect, diagnose, and resolve technical issues, crashes, and errors through crash reporting and error tracking services.
  • Advertising: To display advertisements within the App via Google AdMob. Ad data helps serve relevant ads and measure their effectiveness.
  • Analytics: To understand user behavior patterns, popular features, and usage trends so we can prioritize development efforts and improve the App.
  • In-App Purchases: To manage and validate in-app purchases and subscription status through RevenueCat.
  • Remote Configuration: To dynamically adjust app settings, feature flags, and content delivery through Firebase Remote Config without requiring an app update.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party.

Data may be shared in the following limited circumstances:

  • With Third-Party Service Providers: As described in Section 4, data is shared with integrated services solely for the purposes of app functionality, analytics, advertising, and crash reporting.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required to do so by law, regulation, legal process, or government request.
  • Protection of Rights: We may disclose information when we believe it is necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others, investigate fraud, or respond to a government request.

6. Data Retention

  • Local Game Data: Stored on your device indefinitely until you uninstall the App or manually clear app data. We do not have access to locally stored game data.
  • Analytics Data: Retained by Google Firebase for up to 14 months, after which it is automatically aggregated and anonymized.
  • Crash Reports: Retained by Sentry for up to 90 days, after which they are automatically deleted.
  • Advertising Data: Retention periods are managed by Google AdMob according to their data retention policies.
  • Purchase Data: Retained by RevenueCat as long as necessary to manage your purchases and subscriptions.

7. Data Security

Your data security is one of our highest priorities. We have designed Football Legend with a security-first approach and have implemented multiple layers of protection to ensure your information remains safe at every stage — from collection to storage and transmission. Below is an overview of the comprehensive security measures we have in place:

  • Encrypted Communications: All data transmitted between the App and any remote services is protected using industry-standard HTTPS/TLS encryption protocols. This ensures that your information cannot be intercepted or read by unauthorized parties during transit.
  • Trusted Third-Party Partners: We exclusively integrate with globally recognized and industry-leading third-party services — including Google Firebase, Google AdMob, Sentry, and RevenueCat. Each of these providers maintains world-class security infrastructure, employs dedicated security teams, and undergoes regular independent security audits and compliance certifications (such as SOC 2, ISO 27001, and GDPR compliance).
  • Data Minimization: We follow the principle of data minimization, meaning we only collect the absolute minimum amount of data required to provide the App's core functionality. By limiting what we collect, we inherently reduce any potential security risk.
  • Local-First Storage: Your game progress, team data, match history, and personal game settings are stored directly on your device. This means your most important data never leaves your phone unless you explicitly choose to sync it, giving you full control over your game information.
  • Regular Security Updates: We actively monitor all third-party libraries and dependencies used in the App for known security vulnerabilities. We promptly apply patches and updates to keep Football Legend protected against the latest threats.
  • No Sensitive Data Storage: Football Legend does not collect, store, or process any sensitive personal information such as passwords, payment card details, government IDs, or biometric data. Any payment processing for in-app purchases is handled entirely by the Google Play Store's secure payment infrastructure — we never see or have access to your payment details.
  • Access Controls: Access to any backend systems and analytics dashboards is strictly limited to authorized development personnel only, and protected by strong authentication measures.

We are committed to continuously improving our security practices and staying up-to-date with the latest security standards to ensure that your experience with Football Legend is safe and secure.
